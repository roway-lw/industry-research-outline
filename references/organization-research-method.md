# Single-Unit Cooperation Research Method

Use this guide when the user chooses `具体单位/机构业务调研大纲`.

## Core Purpose

Single-unit research is not a generic background investigation. Its purpose is to identify how one unit can cooperate with the industry-brain project.

Focus on:

- data sharing
- system/API connection
- service co-building
- field scenario pilots
- enterprise service adoption
- resource import
- joint operation
- benchmark case creation

## Two Output Structures

Generate one of two structures according to the unit category.

### Enterprise Structure

Use for chain-owner enterprises, leading enterprises, manufacturing SMEs, supporting-service enterprises, and digital benchmark enterprises.

Output sections:

1. 交流背景及目标
2. 企业基本情况
3. 企业经营与业务现状
   - 采购与供应链
   - 生产与交付
   - 销售与市场
   - 数字化与数据基础
4. 核心痛点与需求
5. 产业大脑合作方向
6. 重点交流问题清单
7. 需补充资料清单

Question focus:

- What role does the enterprise play in the local industry chain?
- What are its urgent problems in procurement, sales, financing, employment, energy, logistics, certification, policy service, and digital transformation?
- Can it become a service user, scenario pilot, benchmark case, chain-owner partner, or data source?
- Which data or systems can support enterprise profile, supply-demand matching, financial services, policy services, or industry monitoring?
- What concerns does the enterprise have about platform use, data sharing, and service charging?

### Ecosystem Institution Structure

Use for associations, alliances, institutes, innovation platforms, digital-transformation service organizations, talent platforms, financial institutions, logistics platforms, testing/certification agencies, park operators, and other non-enterprise ecosystem partners.

Output sections:

1. 交流背景及目标
2. 机构基本情况
3. 机构资源与服务能力
   - 数据资源
   - 服务能力
   - 平台系统能力
4. 现有业务与运营模式
5. 产业大脑合作方向
6. 重点交流问题清单
7. 需补充资料清单

Question focus:

- What resources, data, services, systems, experts, members, activities, or enterprise channels can the institution provide?
- Can it support data sharing, API connection, service entrance embedding, joint events, resource import, or special-service zone co-building?
- What value can the industry brain provide back to the institution?
- What division of work, data security boundary, operation mechanism, and commercial model must be clarified?

## Writing Rules

- Keep the document lighter than a regional industry outline.
- Do not force regional industry sections such as `产业初步认知` or `调研对象及问题清单` onto single-unit documents.
- Write around cooperation possibilities, not general research curiosity.
- Use concrete questions that field researchers can ask directly.
- Separate confirmed facts, assumptions from public information, and items needing on-site confirmation.

## Recommended Attachments

For a single-unit package, use a lighter attachment pack:

1. 单位基础信息记录表
2. 访谈纪要记录表
3. 合作机会梳理表
4. 数据资源与系统对接盘点表
5. 资料收集与行动清单
